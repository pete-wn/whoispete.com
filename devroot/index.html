Hi! I'm Pete.

For career related information, please see my LinkedIn profile.

I'm a photographer, avid reader, parkour instructor, motorcycle ADV rider, computer nerd, and explorer.

I grew up in the Philippines and went to high school in Colombia. I spent most of the late 90's and early 2000's focused on working hard, but decided to go on adventures whenever possible starting in 2008 - since then, I've traveled across most of North America (including every state and almost every province), South America, Europe, significant parts of Asia and been in every ocean except the Southern.

I've been using computers since I was a kid in the early 80's, active on the internet since my first 1200 baud modem in the early 90's, took my first digital photo with an Olympus C-830L on July 24, 1999 and have taken over 125k digital photos with 40+ digital cameras since. UNIX systems have been a daily part of my life since '94 and I've completely lost track of all the different flavors I've worked with, though I still think Windows on the desktop is the way to go. I started programming in Commodore BASIC and dabbled in Instant Pascal before falling in love with Perl - I used Perl or shell most days for the past two decades, though a couple years ago I started switching over to Javascript/NodeJS.
